Organized by Kellen Neville

What is the Summer Speedcubing Series?

On June 15th, we will begin a 10-week online competition amongst Triangle Cubers. Rather than head-to-head, competitors will compete in 5 solves each week (judged by me) and these solves will be calculated against other competitors resulting in points.

    Stage 1 (6 weeks): Participants complete 5 solves each week (you do not have to compete every week if you are not available). Based on these solves, your Ao5, Mo5, and Best Single will be calculated against other competitors earning you points. Only your best 4 weeks (in this stage) count toward your season point total. Earn points each week based on your ranking and time to earn a spot in the playoffs (Stages 2 & 3).
    Stage 2 (2 weeks): Top 16 competitors from stage 1 complete 10 solves each week to add to their season point total.
    Stage 3 (2 weeks): Top 8 competitors from stages 1 & 2 complete 10 solves each week to add to their season point total.
    Finals (4 way head-to-head): Points are reset and top 4 participants compete 1v1v1v1 in 16 solves. Whoever totals the most finals points in the 16 solves is crowned the champion!
